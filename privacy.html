Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit the GTA Study Portal.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require user accounts. The only data collected is standard web analytics and log data (such as IP addresses, browser types, timestamp logs) for troubleshooting and understanding traffic patterns.

2. Third-Party Advertisers (Google AdSense)

We use third-party advertising companies, such as Google AdSense and Media.net, to serve ads when you visit our website.

  • Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

3. Local Storage

We may use local browser storage to save basic UI preferences (such as dark mode preferences or dismissing disclaimers). This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this privacy policy, please reach out via our Contact Page.
