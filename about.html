About GTA Study Portal

The GTA Study Portal is an independent, curated catalogue designed to help international students navigate 2-year, PGWP-eligible college programs physically located in Mississauga and the Greater Toronto Area (GTA).

Our Mission

We believe that navigating post-secondary education and immigration pathways should be transparent and accessible. This tool was built to consolidate highly specialized institutional parameters into a unified, lightning-fast platform.

Independent Status

This resource operates entirely independently. We are not affiliated with Sheridan College, Seneca Polytechnic, Humber Polytechnic, George Brown College, Centennial College, or Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC).
